Motion for a resolution by the committee on student affairs

I, David Rauch,

  • Bearing in mind that eXeLearning is considered unusable by the majority of its users
  • Alarmed by the fact that exercises generated by eXeLearning allow teachers to track student's activities
  • Approving of the digital distribution of learning material
  • Deeply concerned that EYP is not sufficiently supported by the HTBLA Kaindorf
  • Fully believing that a more active EYP-community at the HTBLA Kaindorf will emprove student motivation, behavior and language skills.
  1. Call upon teachers to provide all documents in a standardized elearning platform, such as moodle
  2. Request these documents be provided at all times and in a machine readable format
  3. Urge teachers to limit tracking of student's online activities to the results of single activities, abstaining from tracking the exact time a student spent on an activity.
  4. Encourage the founding of an EYP-club at HTBLA Kaindorf, preferrably in the form of an optional subject that deals with current political issues and debate.
  5. Stronlgy support the funding of EYP activities by both schools and public offices
  6. Strongly encourage students of the HTBLA Kaindorf who are members of the EYP to raise awareness of this activity.
